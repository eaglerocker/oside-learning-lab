# Oside Google Business Profile Mastery

A browser-based mastery curriculum for Google Business Profile optimization, local ranking diagnosis, policy safety, and roofing-client strategy.

## Included

- 12 source-linked modules
- 120 difficult diagnostic questions
- Confidence-based blind-spot detection
- Eight applied labs
- A local-rank grid diagnostic
- Setup, optimization, and client-advisory readiness scoring
- A 30-day improvement plan and Markdown export

## Recommended beginner path

1. Begin with Module 1 rather than the full baseline.
2. Complete each module’s fieldwork.
3. Use **Test this module** immediately afterward.
4. Complete the associated practical labs.
5. Take the full diagnostic after all modules.
6. Complete the roofing-specific capstone.

## Important boundary

Google states that local results are primarily based on relevance, distance, and prominence. No consultant can request, purchase, or guarantee better local ranking. The system teaches compliant optimization, diagnosis, measurement, and client communication.

Progress is stored only in the current browser.
