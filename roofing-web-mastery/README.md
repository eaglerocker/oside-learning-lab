A browser-based mastery system for creating roofing websites that are useful, persuasive, crawlable, indexable, measurable, and eligible for traditional search and AI-powered discovery.

## Included

- 12 source-linked modules
- 120 difficult diagnostic questions
- Eight applied labs
- Local/organic rank-grid diagnostic
- Build, SEO-delivery, and strategy readiness scoring
- Roofing-specific capstone

## Important AEO principle

Google states there are no additional technical requirements, special schema, AI text files, or special markup required for AI Overviews or AI Mode. Existing SEO fundamentals remain the foundation: crawlability, indexability, internal links, textual content, page experience, helpful content, images/video, accurate structured data, and current business information.

Crawler controls are product-specific. For example, Googlebot controls Google Search access, while Google-Extended addresses some other Google AI uses. OpenAI documents OAI-SearchBot for ChatGPT search and GPTBot for potential foundation-model training.

Progress is stored only in the current browser.
